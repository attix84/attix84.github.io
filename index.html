Core Values

Production Technology

- Super-Hydrophobic Layer

Top-quality Super-Hydrophobic material enables perfect protection against water, oil and dust.

Durable anti-scratch technology is also applied for long-lasting use

- Anti-Static Layer

Reduces static to keep dust away from lens surface.

- Anti-Reflection Layers

To ensure the maximum clarity, state-of-the-art material composition program designs DaeMyung Optical’s unique multi-layer AR coating.

- Anti-scratch Layer

DaeMyung Optical has been choosing high-quality Titanium-based hard-coating material Instead of using cheap ones such as Zirconium or Antimon.

The flawless hard-coating provides not only strong anti-scratch function, also extreme adhesion between substrate and other coating layers.
